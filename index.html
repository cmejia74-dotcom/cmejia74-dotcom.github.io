About the engineer

I'm an undergraduate student at Stanford, intending Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Aeronautics & Astronautics. I care about problems where clean geometry meets real physics — deployable structures, attitude control, and anything where a small part has to do a specific job in a demanding environment.

Everything on this site is personal work — designs I model in Fusion 360, sizing problems I sketch out in MATLAB or Python, and small studies I refine for fun outside of class. It's a scratchpad I keep updating as I learn.
